About the Artist

Sawyer Murphy is a 15 year old Texas native who has been drawing for over 6 years. She has 2 blue ribbons from the Rio Grande Valley Livestock show for her art, and has recieved countless recognition from her schools and peers. Sawyer's early work often depicted natural landscapes with native Texas animals. Recently, Sawyer has ventured into the realm of fantasy art with influences from Japanese culture. She currently has a piece on display at Sandra Day O'Connor High School in San Antonio, Texas. Sawyer is currently a full time student but is available for commission. You can contact her about that, or to purchase originals at SawyerLMurphy@gmail.com
